Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Our Commitment: Art Hive takes your privacy seriously. We only collect information necessary to provide and improve our services, and we never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Art Hive, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and/or phone number.
  • Profile Information: Artist name, biography, website links, and social media handles.
  • Content: Artworks you upload, comments, forum posts, likes, and saved preferences.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on site, and navigation patterns.
  • Location Data: General location information to help you find nearby events and exhibitions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To personalize your experience and recommend relevant content
  • To process transactions and manage your account
  • To communicate with you about updates, events, and promotions
  • To improve our website and develop new features
  • To protect against unauthorized access and ensure security

3. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal data:

  • Access and Update: You can review and edit your personal information in your account settings.
  • Communication Preferences: You can opt out of marketing emails at any time.
  • Account Deletion: You may delete your account, which will remove your personal data from our active systems.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

4. Children's Privacy

Art Hive is not intended for users under the age of 13. If you are between 13 and 16, you may only use our services with parental consent. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, we will delete that information promptly [citation:1].

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information :

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Firewalls and secure servers
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited employee access to personal data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information [citation:1][citation:2].

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We may also retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or to comply with legal obligations [citation:1].

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending you an email. Your continued use of Art Hive after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy [citation:1].
