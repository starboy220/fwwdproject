Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Art Hive. If you need further help, feel free to contact us.

How do I join Art Hive?

Click the "Join Now" button at the top right corner of any page. Registration is free and takes less than two minutes. You can sign up as an artist or an art enthusiast.

Can I sell my artwork on Art Hive?

Yes! Art Hive provides a marketplace for artists to showcase and sell their work. We take a minimal commission (5%) on sales to support the platform. You can set your own prices and manage your inventory.

How do I participate in events?

Visit our Events page and browse upcoming exhibitions, workshops, and competitions. Click "Register" on any event to sign up. Some events are free, others may require a ticket.

Is there a fee to use the forum?

No, the forum is completely free for all registered members. You can start discussions, reply to others, and share your work without any cost.

How can I get featured as an artist?

We feature artists based on engagement, quality of work, and community nominations. Keep an active portfolio, participate in discussions, and you might be selected for our homepage or social media.

What payment methods are accepted for purchases?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
